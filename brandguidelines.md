# 🎨 Brand Guidelines (brandguidelines.md)

## 🧾 Product Name

LED Shop System

---

## 🎯 Design Philosophy

* Simple
* Fast
* Clean
* Non-technical user friendly

---

## 🎨 Color Palette

### Primary

#2563EB (Blue)

### Secondary

#0F172A (Dark)

### Background

#F8FAFC (Light Gray)

### Success

#22C55E (Green)

### Danger

#EF4444 (Red)

### Warning

#F59E0B (Yellow)

---

## 🖋️ Typography

* Font: Inter
* Headings: Bold
* Body: Regular

---

## 📐 Layout

* Sidebar navigation (left)
* Main content area
* Top navbar

---

## 🧩 Components

### Buttons

* Primary: Blue
* Secondary: Outline
* Rounded corners

---

### Cards

* Soft shadow
* Padding: 16–24px
* Rounded corners

---

### Tables

* Clean grid
* Zebra rows
* Sticky headers

---

### Forms

* Large inputs
* Clear labels
* Minimal fields

---

## 📊 Dashboard UI

* Cards for:

  * Total Sales
  * Total Stock
  * Low Stock Alerts

---

## 📱 Responsiveness

* Tablet + Mobile friendly
* Collapse sidebar on mobile

---

## ✨ UX Principles

* Minimize clicks
* Fast data entry
* Clear feedback
* Avoid clutter

---

## 🚫 Avoid

* Complex UI
* Too many fields
* Overloaded screens

---

## ✅ Goal

A system that even a non-technical shop owner can use without training.
